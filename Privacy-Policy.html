Privacy Policy for ChinaBizCheck.org

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

At ChinaBizCheck.org ("we", "our", or "us"), we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you purchase our supplier verification services.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the information necessary to fulfill your order and legal obligations:

  • Order Information: Your name and email address (provided via PayPal/Stripe) to deliver the digital PDF report.
  • Target Information: The name and details of the Chinese supplier you request us to verify.
  • Payment Information: All payments are processed securely by PayPal or Credit Card Gateways. We do not store or have access to your full credit card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To conduct the due diligence background check and generate the report.
  • To deliver the final PDF report via secure links (e.g., DocSend).
  • To communicate with you regarding your order status.
  • To prevent fraud and resolve disputes (e.g., retaining delivery logs).

3. Cookies and Third-Party Tracking

We use cookies to improve your experience and analyze site traffic.

  • Google Analytics & Google Ads: We use these tools to understand user behavior and measure the effectiveness of our advertising. These third parties may use cookies to track site usage anonymously.
  • Conversion Tracking: We may track actions such as "Purchase" or "Add to Cart" to optimize our Google Ads performance.

You can opt-out of cookies via your browser settings at any time.

4. Data Protection & Retention

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Verification reports and access logs are retained for a period of 180 days for dispute resolution purposes, after which they may be archived or deleted.
